An interactive generative audio-visual synth. Click and hold to grow glowing musical metaballs. Drag while holding to steer; release to launch with momentum.

  • Drag while holding to move the growing blob; release imparts velocity from your motion.
  • Collide blobs to synthesize chime chords.
  • Autoplay generates ambient notes in the background.
  • Keyboard Shortcuts: [R] clear blobs | [C] toggle controls | [L] toggle logs.
